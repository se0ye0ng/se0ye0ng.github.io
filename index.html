About

Bridging physics, signals, and intelligence — building AI that understands and predicts complex real-world systems.

I'm a master's researcher in AI at Ewha Womans University, specializing in geometric deep learning, spatio-temporal modeling, and AI for Science.

My research spans graph neural networks, physics-informed learning, and their application to large-scale physical and human systems — from biomechanical deformation to nation-scale EV charging demand.

Alongside research, I've shipped production AI across three startups, most recently as Founding AI Engineer at VStdy.
